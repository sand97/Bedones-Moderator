## During the Campaign: Minute-by-Minute Protocol

### Hours 0-2 (CRITICAL phase)

**Minute 0**:

## The 7 Errors That Blow Up Your CPA

### 1. Leaving scams/spam visible > 5 min

**Impact**: -40 to -70% conversions
**Cost**: Reputation + lost sales + scammed customers blaming you

### 2. Answering questions after 30 minutes

**African reality**: Impatient prospects contact a competitor in 15-20 min.
**Loss**: 65% of prospects not answered quickly buy elsewhere.

### 3. Ignoring delivery questions

**Statistic**: 47% of African comments are about delivery/payment.
Ignoring = signal "I'm not serious".

### 4. No pinned comment with official contacts

**Result**: Confusion, repetitive questions, easier scams.

### 5. Not removing malicious negative reviews

**Difference**:
- Legitimate criticism â†’ Respond publicly, resolve
- Competitor attack â†’ Hide + report

### 7. No post-campaign analysis

**Loss**: Repeat the same errors, don't capitalize on what works.
