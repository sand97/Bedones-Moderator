## Step 4: Manage Sensitive Cases with Smart Escalation

### Cases to NEVER automate

**Serious complaints**:
- Triggers: "scam", "fraud", "complaint", "lawyer", "police", "court"
- Action: Immediate alert + human reply < 30 min

**Product issues**:
- "Doesn't work", "defective", "broken", "damaged"
- Temporary reply: "Sorry for the inconvenience. A manager will contact you within 15 min for a quick solution."

**Custom requests**:
- "Special order", "custom-made", "large quantity"
- Reply: "Great! For this specific request, contact [manager name] directly: [WhatsApp]"

### Universal exit line

## Step 5: Measure and Optimize Continuously

### KPIs to track weekly

1. **FAQ coverage rate**: % of questions resolved by FAQ
   - Target: > 65%

2. **Average response time**
   - Before FAQ vs After FAQ
   - Target: < 2 minutes

3. **Click-through rate on links**
   - How many click WhatsApp/Site after reply
   - Target: > 40%

4. **Uncovered questions**
   - New questions appearing frequently
   - Action: Add to FAQ if > 10 occurrences/week

5. **Escalation rate**
   - % of conversations requiring human intervention
   - Target: < 25%

6. **Satisfaction rate**
   - Measured via "thanks", positive emojis, purchases
   - Target: > 80%

### Optimization routine

**Weekly (15 min)**:
- Identify 3-5 new recurring questions
- Write answers and add triggers
- Check link click rate (replace if < 20%)

**Monthly (1h)**:
- Analyze false positives (bad auto replies)
- Adjust overly broad triggers
- A/B test 2 versions of important replies
- Update prices/info if changes
