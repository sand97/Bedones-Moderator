## The 3 Families of Replies That Convert

### 1. Informational replies

Price, features, payment options, delivery location. The clearer it is, the easier the purchase.

### 2. Reassurance replies

Guarantee, customer reviews, return policy, real timelines. They reduce fears.

### 3. Action-oriented replies

Order link, WhatsApp button, private message with a simple form.

## Smart Triggers Without Spam

### Keywords optimized by intent

**Purchase intent** (highest priority):
- "how much", "price", "cost"
- "order", "buy", "take"
- "available", "stock"

**Information intent**:
- "delivery", "deliver", "send"
- "payment", "orange money", "mtn"
- "where", "address", "location"

**Hesitation intent** (needs reassurance):
- "original", "real", "authentic"
- "quality", "guaranteed", "reliable"

### Essential anti-spam rules

1. **Natural delay**: 3-8 seconds before reply
2. **Frequency limit**: Max 1 auto reply / 15 min per person
3. **Double-question detection**: If 2+ questions, combined reply or human escalation
4. **Human exit**: Always propose "Questions? WhatsApp [link]"

### Complex cases to escalate

**DO NOT automate if**:
- Complaint or dissatisfaction detected
- Very specific/personalized question
- Price negotiation
- Large quantity request (B2B)
