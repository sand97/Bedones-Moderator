## Identify Your Sensitive Hours

### Analyze your comment peaks

Use Facebook Insights to identify:
- **Peak hours**: Generally 19h-22h in francophone Africa
- **High-audience days**: Wednesday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
- **High-engagement posts**: Sponsored posts, launches, promotions

**Example - Abidjan fashion shop**:
- Absolute peak: Friday 20h-23h (32% of weekly comments)
- Secondary peak: Sunday 18h-21h (18% of comments)
- Low hours: Tuesday-Thursday 10h-14h (8% of comments)

### Map purchase intent

Identify when "hot" questions arrive:
- Price/availability questions: Mostly evenings and weekends
- Customer service questions: More during the day
- Toxic comments: Peaks after 21h

## Keep a Human in the Loop (Hybrid Model)

### Daily review of hidden comments

**Morning routine (15 min)**:
- Review automatically hidden comments
- Validate or restore if false positive
- Adjust rules if needed

### Adjust rules each week

**Friday routine (30 min)**:
- Analyze false positive/negative rate
- Enrich the automated reply base
- Add new detected FAQs

### Manual intervention for disputes

**Auto-escalation criteria**:
- Amount at stake > 50,000 CFA
- Mention of "lawyer", "complaint", "police"
- VIP customer identified
- Very negative sentiment

**Required human response time**: < 30 minutes.

## Measure the Performance of Your 24/7 Strategy

### Essential KPIs

1. **Average response time by time slot**
   - Overall target: < 15 min
   - Peak hours: < 5 min
   - Off-peak hours: < 30 min

2. **Resolution rate without human**
   - Target: > 70%
   - Measure: % of conversations closed by AI alone

3. **Conversion rate by time slot**
   - Compare daytime vs evening vs weekend conversion
   - Identify the most profitable hours

4. **Customer satisfaction score**
   - Measure via "thanks", "perfect", positive emojis
   - Target: > 85% positive sentiment

5. **ROI by time slot**
   - Revenue generated vs availability cost
   - Proves 24/7 profitability
