## The 6 Essential Rules Every Charter Must Contain

### 1. Respect for People and Prohibition of Insults

Clearly specify:
- No personal attacks
- No threats or harassment
- No mockery targeting appearance, origin or beliefs

**Example**: "Insults, threats and personal attacks will be deleted without notice."

### 2. Prohibition of Hate Speech

Specify that content targeting:
- Ethnic origin
- Religion
- Gender or orientation
- Any other form of discrimination

will be deleted immediately.

### 3. No Fraudulent Links or Scams

Protect your community by prohibiting:
- Fake contests
- Money or personal information requests
- Suspicious or unverified shortened links
- Promises of quick gains

**Common Case in Africa**: "Easy money" scams and fake WhatsApp Business represent 35% of fraudulent comments on African pages.

### 4. No Unauthorized Advertising

Clarify that only your official partners can:
- Promote products or services
- Post commercial links
- Recruit or sell

### 5. No Repetitive Spam

Define spam as:
- Identical messages posted multiple times
- Repetitive meaningless emojis
- Copy-paste of promotional texts

### 6. Respect for Privacy

Prohibit:
- Publishing phone numbers
- Exposing others' personal information
- Screenshots of private conversations

## How to Apply the Charter Without Creating Conflict

### Step 1: Educational Reminder First

Before deleting, respond:
"Hello! This type of comment goes against our charter. Please rephrase respectfully."

### Step 2: Hide to Avoid Escalation

For borderline content, hide rather than delete. The comment remains visible to its author but not to others.

### Step 3: Documented Deletion

For serious violations:
- Delete immediately
- Keep a screenshot
- Note reason in your log

### Step 4: Block as Last Resort

Reserved for repeat offenders or confirmed malicious accounts.

## Stay Consistent in Application

**Common Error**: Applying different rules based on:
- Person's notoriety
- Your personal relationships
- Your mood of the day

**Good Practice**: Treat all comments by same criteria. An influencer or partner is not above rules.

## Measure Your Charter's Effectiveness

Track these indicators:
- **Compliance Rate**: % of comments respecting the charter
- **Challenge Rate**: % of users challenging a deletion
- **Moderation Time**: before/after implementation
- **General Sentiment**: evolution of comment tone

**Benchmark**: A well-applied charter reduces moderation time by 40 to 60% on average.
