## The Ivorian Legal Framework in 2026

### Cybersecurity and Cybercrime Law

**Main text**: Law No. 2013-451 of June 19, 2013 and recent updates.

**What it says**:
- Hosts (including page owners) have a moderation obligation
- Removal deadline for illegal content: 24-48h after reporting
- Criminal liability possible if negligence is proven

### Influence threshold and enhanced obligations

**From ~25,000 followers**:
- Enhanced compliance obligation
- Declaration to ARTCI recommended
- Obligation of means (not just results) for moderation

**Between 5,000 and 25,000**:
- Standard obligations
- Reasonable diligence expected

**Below 5,000**:
- Lower risk but not zero
- Good faith required

### Types of content particularly targeted

1. **Hate speech**: Ethnic, religious, regional
2. **Misinformation**: Public health, elections, security
3. **Violent content**: Calls for violence, threats
4. **Harm to minors**: Enhanced protection
5. **Defamation**: Especially against institutions/personalities

### Sanctions in 2026

**Administrative sanctions (ARTCI)**:
- Warning
- Fine: 500,000 to 5,000,000 CFA
- Temporary page suspension
- Permanent closure (serious cases)

**Criminal sanctions (Justice)**:
- Fines up to 10 million CFA
- Prison sentences: 1 month to 5 years depending on severity
- Ban on practicing

**Commercial sanctions**:
- Loss of partnerships (brands avoid risks)
- Reputation damage
- Difficulty monetizing

## 8 Priority and Actionable Protection Measures

**Minimum content**:

- Consequences (hide, delete, block)

### 2. Moderate continuously with proof of diligence

**Reasonable diligence** = Prove that you did your best.

**Daily checklist**:
- Morning (30 min): Review overnight comments
- Midday (15 min): Quick check
- Evening (30 min): Final review + report

**Maximum acceptable delay**: 2h for sensitive content, 24h for the rest.

### 3. Document ALL your moderation actions

**What to keep (12 months minimum)**:
- Screenshot of the comment
- Date/time of detection
- Action taken (hidden/deleted/blocked)
- Reason (which rule was violated)
- Name of the person who made the decision

**Recommended format**: Google Sheets or Notion with columns:
| Date | Time | Author | Comment | Action | Reason | Responsible |

**Use case**: In case of audit or dispute, this document proves your good faith.

### 4. Train your team or CM on Ivorian specifics

**Mandatory training** (2h minimum):
- CI-specific legal framework
- Particularly sensitive content in CI
- Moderation process to follow
- Escalation for legal cases

**Validation test**: Quiz to verify understanding.

### 5. Set up an automatic alert system

**Action**: Immediate push alert + automatic quarantine.

### 6. Take out professional liability insurance

**Why**: Covers legal defense costs + possible fines.

**Cost**: 150,000 - 400,000 CFA/year depending on revenue.

**Return**: Peace of mind + coverage up to 50 million CFA.

### 7. Have an identified legal counsel

**No need for a permanent lawyer**, but:

### 8. Generate monthly compliance reports

**Report content**:

- Number of moderated comments
- Types of violations detected

- Improvement actions

**Usefulness**: Proof of professionalism + basis for continuous improvement.
