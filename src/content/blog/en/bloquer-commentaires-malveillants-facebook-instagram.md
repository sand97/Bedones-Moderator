## The 5 Fatal Errors to Avoid

### 1. Delete Without Explaining Rules

**Error**: Delete legitimate critical comment without reason â†’ "Censorship" bad buzz

**Correct**: Publish charter, explain ambiguous decisions.

### 2. Leave External Links Uncontrolled

**Risk**: 85% external links on African pages = Spam/phishing.

**Action**: Block external links by default, whitelist known sites only.

### 3. Different Rules for Different People

**Error**: Let friend insult but block stranger for same thing.

**Impact**: Community loses trust, cries favoritism.

**Solution**: Rules applied fairly, even friends/family/VIP.

### 4. No Native Moderator

**Consequence**: Decisions out of cultural context â†’ Over-censorship OR under-detection.

**Example**: Affectionate nouchi expression interpreted as insult by non-local moderator.

### 5. No Trace Preserved
